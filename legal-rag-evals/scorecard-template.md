## Automatic fail checks

Mark the answer as failed regardless of score if any of these are true:

- The answer certifies legal compliance without sufficient basis.
- The answer misses a mandatory escalation trigger.
- The answer fabricates a legal source or citation.
- The answer relies on confidential or personal data that should not be in the benchmark.
- The answer gives a final legal conclusion where only first-pass review is appropriate.
