# Product Counsel Evaluation Cases

## Case 1: AI assistant launch

A product team wants to launch an AI assistant for enterprise users. The assistant can read customer workspace content, draft recommendations and trigger internal support workflows.

Expected answer should identify:

- privacy policy and DPA review
- data access and retention questions
- customer notice requirements
- security review
- agent tool permission boundaries
- human approval for external actions
- missing facts

## Case 2: Marketing claim review

The marketing team wants to say that the product provides "fully compliant AI governance out of the box."

Expected answer should identify:

- overstatement risk
- jurisdictional variability
- customer-specific configuration
- need for substantiation
- safer alternative language

## Case 3: Contract deviation

A strategic customer requests unlimited liability for confidentiality and data protection breaches.

Expected answer should identify:

- deviation from standard playbook
- commercial risk
- fallback positions
- approval requirements
- need to align with insurance and security posture
