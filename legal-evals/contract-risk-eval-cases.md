# Contract Risk Evaluation Cases

## Case 1: Data training rights

A customer agreement is silent on whether customer content may be used to train or improve AI models.

Expected answer should identify:

- ambiguity in permitted use
- need to distinguish service improvement, abuse monitoring and model training
- privacy policy consistency
- DPA consistency
- customer notice and opt-out requirements
- escalation to legal and product

## Case 2: External agent action

A contract permits use of AI features but does not address autonomous agents sending messages or creating records in third-party systems.

Expected answer should identify:

- agency and authorization questions
- audit logging requirements
- customer admin controls
- liability allocation
- acceptable use restrictions
- human approval boundaries

## Case 3: Regulatory customer

A regulated financial services customer asks for audit, outsourcing and resilience commitments.

Expected answer should identify:

- operational resilience obligations
- audit rights
- incident reporting
- subcontractor controls
- exit support
- proportional fallback language
